It is with a heavy heart that the Find Your Activity team have had to close our startup and our journey together is over for now.

The COVID-19 lockdowns impacted our growth plans, but we continued on providing a valuable service to many activity providers across the UK.

As they say, better to have tried and failed than to have never tried at all.

Even though this might be considered a failure, to us it will always remain an incredible experience. The team has grown in more ways than we could have imagined, and we have memories that will live on forever.

We have thoroughly enjoyed supporting the amazing activity providers by sharing their services and connecting individuals with their perfect activities. We wish them all the success in the future driving their own businesses forward.
